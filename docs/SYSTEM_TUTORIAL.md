## ğŸ“ role: Teacher

Teachers focus on the day-to-day academic progress of their assigned students.

### 1. Attendance
- **Mark Attendance**: Select your class and section to record daily attendance (Present, Absent, Late, Excused).

### 2. Assessments & Grading
- **Marks Entry**: Enter scores for Classwork, Homework, Mid-terms, and Finals.
- **BECE Grading**: For JHS 3, enter grades (A1 to F9) and calculate aggregates.
- **Report Cards**: Generate and print terminal reports for students in your class.

### 3. Classroom Tools
- **Homework**: Post assignments with descriptions and deadlines for students and parents to see.
- **Timetable**: View your assigned teaching schedule.
