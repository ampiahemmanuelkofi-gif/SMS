# School Management System User Manual

## 1. Getting Started
To access the system, go to `http://localhost/sch` and log in with your credentials.
- **Default Admin:** admin / admin123

## 2. Student Admission
1. Go to **Students** > **Admission Form**.
2. Fill in student details, upload a photo, and assign a class/section.
3. Click **Save Student**.

## 3. Attendance
1. Go to **Attendance** > **Mark Attendance**.
2. Select Class, Section, and Date.
3. Mark students as Present, Absent, etc., and click **Save All**.

## 4. Academic Structure
1. **Academic Periods**: Set the current year and term.
2. **Classes & Sections**: Manage the school rooms.
3. **Teacher Assignments**: Link teachers to subjects and sections.

## 5. Assessments & BECE
1. **Marks Entry**: Enter class scores and exam marks for any subject.
2. **BECE Results**: For JHS 3 students, enter WAEC grades to calculate aggregates automatically.
3. **Report Cards**: View a student's profile to generate and print their terminal report.

## 6. Fee Management
1. **Fee Structure**: Define how much each class pays per term.
2. **Collect Fees**: Record payments and print official receipts.
3. **Balances**: Track outstanding fees across the school.

## 7. Promotions
1. Go to **Academic Structure** > **Promotions**.
2. Select the "Source Section" and "Target Section".
3. Check the students to promote and click **Promote Selected Students**.
4. The system logs these movements in the promotion history.

## 8. Homework & Assignments
1. **Teachers**: Select your class/subject, enter the title and description, and set a deadline.
2. **Parents**: Log in to view a list of homework assigned to your children.
3. **Admins**: View all postings via the Homework menu.

## 9. Parent Portal
1. Guardians can log in using their email address.
2. The dashboard shows an overview of their children's performance, attendance, and fees.
3. "My Children" provides detailed terminal reports and assessment scores.

## 10. System Settings
- **School Configuration**: Update school name, contact info, and logo via **Settings**.
- **User Management**: Manage staff accounts and roles.

## 11. Security & Maintenance
- **Logout**: Always click the Logout button when finished.
- **Passwords**: Change your default password in the **Profile** section.
- **Backups**: Use PHPMyAdmin to export a weekly SQL backup of the school database.
